1. Project Introduction

The hospitality industry is rapidly evolving, with digital solutions becoming the backbone of customer experience and operational management. The Sea Horizon Hotel Management System is proposed as a comprehensive web-based platform designed to streamline hotel operations, from room reservations to administrative reporting.

This project aims to bridge the gap between manual management processes and high-end enterprise solutions, providing a scalable and user-friendly tool for small to medium-sized hotels.

2. Problem Statement

Many independent hotels still rely on manual ledgers or fragmented spreadsheets to manage their bookings. This leads to several critical issues:

  • Difficulty in tracking real-time room availability.
  • High risk of overbooking or double-booking errors.
  • Inability to generate comprehensive occupancy and revenue reports.
  • Inefficient communication between the hotel and prospective guests.

3. Project Objectives

The primary goal of this project is to develop a functional and secure Hotel Management System. Specific objectives include:

  • Automation: To automate the room booking process, reducing manual intervention.
  • Inventory Management: To provide a real-time view of room availability across different categories.
  • Reporting: To enable administrators to generate performance reports (bookings between dates).
  • User Experience: To create an intuitive interface for guests to browse and book rooms easily.

4. Scope & Limitations

System Scope

The project will cover the development of two primary interfaces: a Guest-facing portal for bookings and an Admin-facing dashboard for management. It includes room categorization, facility listing, and enquiry management.

Limitations

  • The system will not process real-time financial transactions (simulated booking only).
  • Manual cancellation by admins is required for this version.
  • Hosted on a local server environment (XAMPP/WAMP) for current development.

5. Methodology & Tech Stack

The project follows the Waterfall Model of software development, ensuring structured progression through Requirements, Design, Development, and Testing phases.

Frontend

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, and Google Fonts for a premium UI.

Backend

PHP 8.2 for robust server-side logic and session management.

Database

MySQL for relational data storage and structured querying.

Architecture

Client-Server model with a dedicated Admin subdirectory for security.

6. System Modules

User Side

  • Online Registration: Secure guest sign-up and login.
  • Room Selection: Categorized room browsing with detailed descriptions.
  • My Bookings: Dashboard for guests to view their reservation history.
  • Enquiry Form: Direct communication channel with hotel management.

Admin Side

  • Dashboard Stats: Quick overview of users, bookings, and enquiries.
  • Booking Management: Review, approve, or reject reservation requests.
  • CMS: Manage front-end content including About Us and Facilities.
  • Reports: Generate filtered booking reports for business analysis.
